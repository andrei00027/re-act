# Privacy Policy for MomentumFlow

**Last Updated:** December 16, 2024

## Introduction

MomentumFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our habit tracking application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** When you sign in with Apple ID, we receive your name and email (if you choose to share it)
- **Habit Data:** Information about your habits, including names, icons, completion history, and settings
- **Health Data:** If you grant permission, we access your Apple Health data (workouts, steps, sleep) to automatically mark habits as complete

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** We may collect basic device information for app functionality (iOS version, device type)
- **Usage Data:** Local analytics about how you use the app (stored only on your device)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide core habit tracking functionality
- Sync your data across your Apple devices via iCloud
- Automatically complete habits based on Apple Health data
- Send you local reminder notifications
- Improve app performance and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All your habit data is stored locally on your device using secure iOS storage (AsyncStorage)
- Apple ID tokens are stored in iOS Secure Store (encrypted)

### iCloud Sync
- If enabled, your habit data is synced to your private iCloud storage
- Only you can access this data through your iCloud account
- We do not have access to your iCloud data

### Apple Health
- Health data is only read from your device with your explicit permission
- We never write or modify your Apple Health data
- Health data is not stored or transmitted outside your device

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.**

The only data sharing occurs through:
- **iCloud:** Your data syncs to your personal iCloud account (controlled by you)
- **Apple:** Sign in with Apple provides authentication (covered by Apple's privacy policy)

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View all your habit data within the app
- **Delete:** Delete individual habits or all data at any time
- **Export:** Request data export (contact support)
- **Opt-out:** Disable iCloud sync or Apple Health integration at any time
- **Sign Out:** Remove your account from the device

## Children's Privacy

MomentumFlow is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services We Use
- **Sign in with Apple:** Authentication (see [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/))
- **iCloud:** Data synchronization (see [iCloud Privacy](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202303))
- **Apple Health:** Health data integration (stored locally only)
- **Push Notifications:** Local reminder notifications (no data transmitted)

## Data Retention

- Habit data is retained until you delete it or remove the app
- iCloud backup data follows your iCloud retention settings
- Removing the app deletes all local data

## International Users

If you use MomentumFlow outside your country, your data may be transferred to and stored in iCloud servers in various locations according to Apple's iCloud infrastructure.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)

## GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Union:
- **Legal Basis:** We process data based on your consent and contractual necessity
- **Data Controller:** You are the controller of your habit data
- **Right to Access:** You can access all your data in the app
- **Right to Erasure:** You can delete all data at any time
- **Data Portability:** Contact us for data export

## Security Measures

We implement security measures including:
- iOS Secure Store for sensitive data
- Encrypted iCloud transmission
- No server-side data storage (local-first architecture)
- Regular security updates

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

**Email:** support@momentumflow.app
**App:** Use "Contact Support" in Settings
